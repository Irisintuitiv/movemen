Is MOVEMEN
for you?

Before joining, take a moment to reflect honestly. MOVEMEN is not about becoming a professional dancer. It is about becoming more free, grounded, confident, and connected in yourself.

If reading these questions makes you think: "Yes — that's me."
Then MOVEMEN is likely exactly what you need.

02 — Self-reflection

Do you find it difficult to fully relax, express yourself, or be fully seen around others?

MOVEMEN is for you.

This program is likely for you if

  • You are tired of holding yourself back
  • You want more confidence and freedom in your body and mind
  • You overthink social situations or self-expression
  • You want to feel more grounded, masculine, and alive
  • You want to stop caring so much about judgment
  • You are open-minded and willing to grow
  • You know deep down there is more inside of you
  • You are willing to be uncomfortable in order to transform

It may not be for you if

  • You only want choreography or technical dance training
  • You are unwilling to step outside your comfort zone
  • You are closed off to self-reflection or personal growth
  • You want change without participation or effort
  • You are not willing to be challenged

What would your life look like if you finally stopped holding yourself back?

And what could become possible if you fully trusted yourself, your body, and your expression?
